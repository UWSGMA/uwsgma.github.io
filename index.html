About Us

We are a community of queer astronomers and allies dedicated to supporting and promoting LGBTQ+ individuals in the field of astronomy. Our mission is to foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity.

Events

Join us for monthly stargazing nights, workshops, and community-building events. Stay tuned for our upcoming events and collaborations with other queer science groups!
